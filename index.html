About me

My name is Robel Fikru, and I am from Ethiopia. I will be 23 years old in April. As of January 2023, I have joined CYF to learn web development. I have always been interested in computers, but I never got around to start coding. I believe that this will be a life-changing opportunity for me.

Here are a few cool things about me

Fitness

There are many things that I have given up on throughout my existence, but one thing that I have not given up on is my fitness. In addition to the obvious physical benefits of going to the gym, it has helped me to become more consistent and disciplined in other aspects of my life.

Photography

I am not a professional photographer, but I enjoy taking pictures and videos and editing them on Photoshop and Premiere Pro. Although it is a hobby, I would like to work on improving my skills.

Anime

You're probably wondering why a 22-year-old would watch shows made for kids. I generally enjoy watching movies ranging from horror to romance, but recently, I've become obsessed with watching cartoons fight, rage, cry, etc. Trust me, if you try it, you would be too.
